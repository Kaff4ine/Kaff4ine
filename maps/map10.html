kz_knuckle

Counter-Strike: Source

Released: 2025-07-29





kz_knuckle is a pretty simple and short kreedz (climb) map for cs source that I made in around a month or so.

It's around tier 2 in difficulty, and the map is really heavy on a brutalistic/broken down kind of aesthetic.

The whole vibe/layout is also heavily inspired by the indie game 'White Knuckle' hence the name,




One my favorite parts of the whole map is probably how I got the starting room of the map to connect to the end of the map through the ceilling (kind of like some spots in dark souls lol)
